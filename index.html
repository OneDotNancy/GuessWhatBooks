Welcome to Guess What and Then What books--two series written by award-winning author, Nancy O’Neill, featuring children’s picture books plus activity and coordinating notebooks. Both series are designed for children ages three and older. All books are available on Amazon in paperback and e-book.

Then What books are full of imagination and silliness where wacky animal adventures come alive and anything is possible. The repeating If-Then rhyming format helps young children recognize patterns and gives the opportunity to use predictive skills to guess what happens if... Colorful, playful--and yes--totally silly illustrations enhance these books. Look for more books in this series.

Guess What books are written in four-line rhyming clues that describe people, places, and things within a specific theme. Little-known facts and many familiar ones lead children on a fun guessing game and learning experience. After the child listens to or reads the clues, turning the page reveals a colorful illustration of the object which lets them know if they guessed correctly.

Each book features illustrations done by Kids Around the World whose artwork was selected through a worldwide contest. The contests were open to children ages 5-17. A list of the winning illustrators for each book can be found on the Illustrators tab and inside each book.
