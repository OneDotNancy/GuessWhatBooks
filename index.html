Welcome to Guess What books, a series written by award-winning author, Nancy O’Neill featuring children’s picture books plus activity and blank books. All of the Guess What books are available on Amazon. If you want to order autographed copies, please email me.

Click on a book to purchase on Amazon

Guess What picture books are designed for children ages three and older. Each book in the series includes simple, yet clever, four-line rhyming clues that describe people, places, and things within a specific theme. Little known facts and many familiar ones lead children on a fun guessing game and learning experience. After the child listens to or reads the clues, turning the page reveals a colorful illustration of the object which lets them know if they guessed correctly.

Each book features illustrations done by Kids Around the World whose artwork was selected through a worldwide contest. The contests were open to children ages 5-17. A list of the winning illustrators for each book can be found on the Illustrators tab.

The combination of the rhyming clues and illustrations by talented, young artists is what makes these books so unique.
