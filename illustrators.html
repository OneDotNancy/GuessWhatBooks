When I first considered publishing Guess What is on Grandpa’s Farm? I wanted the illustrations to be something special and unique. After thinking about all of the options, I decided I wanted to feature young artists. Not only would each book have something different but it would give children an opportunity to explore creative possibilities in their own lives.

Each book features artwork from Kids Around the World. The selection process was done through an art contest that I held. Unfortunately, only thirteen illustrations were necessary for each book but every child who entered deserved special recognition. This image represents some of the illustrations from the first three books. As you can see, creativity and talent comes in all forms. Each young illustrator has their own unique style.

Illustrations by young artists from Australia, Italy, Poland, India and many US states are featured in the five picture books. The following is a list of the winners from each book, as well as other young artists who submitted their illustrations. This is not a complete list because each student had the option to not have their name included. So with permission ...
